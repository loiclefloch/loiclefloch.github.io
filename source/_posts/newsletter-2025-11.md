- [HP Won Innovation Awards. Then Killed What Made It True.](https://philmckinney.substack.com/p/hp-won-innovation-awards-then-killed) - HP's  innovation was never the result of formal policies like "20% time" but grew from a culture of deep trust, personal investment, and unrestricted access to resources, supported by hands-on leadership. Confusing correlation for causation led companies like Google to copy it while the real engine of creativity - relationships, freedom, and genuine support - was forgotten, proving that sustainable innovation cannot be mandated but must be cultivated through culture.
